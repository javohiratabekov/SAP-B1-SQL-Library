# Customer Queries

## Purpose
SQL queries for customer master data, analysis, and relationship management.

## Query Types
- Customer master list
- Customer credit status
- Customer activity analysis
- Top customers by revenue
- Customer segmentation
- Inactive customer reports

## Example File Names
- `BP_CUST_Customer_Master_List.sql`
- `BP_CUST_Credit_Limit_Status.sql`
- `BP_CUST_Top_Customers_By_Revenue.sql`
- `BP_CUST_Inactive_Customers.sql`
- `BP_CUST_Customer_Activity_Report.sql`

## SAP B1 Tables
Common tables used:
- `OCRD` - Business Partners
- `CRD1` - Business Partner Addresses
- `OINV` - AR Invoice Header
- `ORDR` - Sales Order Header
- `OCPR` - Contact Persons
