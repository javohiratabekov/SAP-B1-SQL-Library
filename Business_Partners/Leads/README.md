# Leads

SAP B1: **Business Partners → Leads**

Prospective customers or vendors not yet converted to active BP accounts.

## Key Tables
- `OCRD` where `CardType = 'L'`
- `OCPR` — Contact Persons

## Converting a Lead
In SAP B1: Open Lead → Business Partner Menu → Convert to Customer/Vendor

## Naming Prefix
`BP_LEAD_` — e.g., `BP_LEAD_Conversion_Rate.sql`, `BP_LEAD_Pipeline_Report.sql`
